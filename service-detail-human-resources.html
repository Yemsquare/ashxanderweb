Background Checks

Background checks are important steps for an organization to take when considering potential candidates for new role. Ash Xander Human Resource Team will conduct these checks on the prospective candidates professional and personal lives in order to make a conclusion on whether the candidate is well fit for the job. There are different types of background checks which we carry out. This includes: verification of past employment, verification of the applicant’s information, verifying the candidate’s references, determining if there are any criminal or civil records pertaining to the candidate.

Recruitment

We are in the business of identifying and recruiting competent professionals that will drive the success of organizations. We are also focused on finding the best suited and qualified candidate either from within or outside the organization for a position. At Ash Xander, we strive to ensure that the best and most qualified candidates are recruited. We look out for candidates who share similar values, visions and goals of our client company. And this is because we are aware that if we get it right at the recruitment level, every other function of Human Resource will run smoothly.
