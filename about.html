First class business solutions

Ash Xander (“AX”) is a global consulting business headquartered in Mauritius with its principal place of business in Lagos. It was incorporated in Mauritius as a Limited company in 2017 under a different business name and was officially renamed as Ash Xander on October 25, 2019.

Ash Xander officially commenced business operations in November 1, 2019 and offers business dealings in Financial Advisory, Human Resources, Public Relations & Branding, Technology.
