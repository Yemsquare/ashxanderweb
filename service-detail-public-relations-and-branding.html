What can I do to create a strong online presence?

  • Improve your google search options by creating a Google My Business Page
  • Build Your Social Media Presence through Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn or TikTok
  • Create an appealing Website
  • Build an Email List and start Email Marketing

What is the best way to gain brand credibility & trust?

  • Have a strong digital footprint
  • Build credibility through Media mentions and news coverage
  • Work on improving your coverage through Search Engine Optimization
  • Constantly push out content that promotes your key messaging to your Audience
  • Always keep your Stakeholders in the loop by informing them of activities through Newsletters, Feedback Forms and getting feedback through Opinion Polls

What do I do to gain relationships with the Media and build visibility?

  • Attend a Media training and learn what to say, how to say it and the tone to say things
  • Start creating a Media List by building your network one at a time
  • Connect with the relevant Media in your niche industry and communicate frequently
  • Learn to celebrate the Media and treat them with respect as they are fellow humans like you
  • Learn to speak the language of the press and do not bombard them with irrelevant press releases, always be strategic and intentional.
